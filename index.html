Vegetarian Kitchen

Suppliers and Consultants to the Catering Industry for over 25 years

Founded in 1987, we are now the UK’s premier specialist distributor of vegetarian and vegan products within the foodservice sector. Our expertise of specialist vegetarian and vegan products enables us to offer expert advice on nutrition, menu planning and not least how to maximise profit potential whilst exceeding your client’s demands for exciting, innovative and healthy menus.

Our Values

MISSION: Our mission – To inspire people to choose a healthier, greener, more compassionate lifestyle through plant-based eating.

VISION: Our vision – We envision a world where people think critically about their food choices, where growing numbers of businesses, community groups, and organizations inspire people to make healthier, greener, more compassionate choices, and where nutritious, sustainable plant-based foods are accessible to all.
